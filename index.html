aMazeGarden

A fully autonomous robotic gardener taking care of your plants and protecting them from extreme weather.

  • Learn more

Automatic Irrigation

Using its suite of many sensors, aMazeGarden monitors all important weather and soil parameters. Using these parameters, it intelligently decides when to water your plants. With aMazeGarden, you can say goodbye to wilted plants!

Protection from hailstorms

aMazeGarden always monitors the weather and is able to detect events such as hailstorms that could damage your plants. After detecting a hailstorm, it promptly deploys a protective cover which blocks the hail. When hail stops, the cover is automatically tucked away.

Always ahead of the weather

In conjunction with its sensors, aMazeGarden also connects to weather forecast services with which it can predict extreme weather events such as high temperatures, incessant rainfalls and thunderstorms. Using this information, aMazeGarden is able to protect your plants in a very efficient way.

Always connected

aMazeGarden is always connected to the cloud enabling you to check on your garden and control it from anywhere in the world.

Wi-Fi Connectivity

aMazeGarden can use Wi-Fi in order to connect to the cloud.

LoRaWAN Network

In remote areas without internet connectivity, aMazeGarden can utilize LoRaWAN, a long range and low power radio network designed especially for IoT devices.
